## Slide 27

Prompt engineering involves crafting specific prompts or  queries to guide the behavior of large language models,

Allowing users to elicit desired responses or  information from the model.

Customization: It enables customization of the model's  responses to align with specific objectives, contexts,  or industries.

Developers can fine-tune the model's responses by  designing prompts that provide clear instructions,  constraints, or examples, influencing the output.

Crafting effective prompts often involves an iterative  process of experimentation and refinement.
